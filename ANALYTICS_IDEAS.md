## Additional Analytics Ideas ðŸ’¡

### Roster & Lineup Analytics
1. **Active Roster Utilization**
   - % of roster spots filled vs empty
   - Average games played per roster spot
   - Bench depth analysis (how many quality players on bench)

2. **Positional Strength**
   - Category strengths by position (PG, SG, SF, PF, C)
   - Positional category rankings (e.g., "Best PG group for assists")
   - Positional minutes distribution

3. **Injury & Availability Impact**
   - Games missed due to injury
   - DNP (Did Not Play) count per team
   - Impact of injured players on category performance

### Category-Specific Analytics
4. **Category Efficiency**
   - Points per minute played
   - Rebounds per game
   - Turnovers per 100 possessions
   - Shooting efficiency (FG%, FT% with volume context)

5. **Category Trends**
   - Week-over-week category improvement/decline
   - Best/worst categories over last 4 weeks
   - Category consistency (variance in category wins)

6. **Category Matchup Analysis**
   - Which categories you're most likely to win/lose against each opponent
   - Category strengths vs specific teams

### Player Performance Analytics
7. **Player Value Analysis**
   - Best/worst performers relative to draft position
   - Waiver wire gems (players added that are performing well)
   - Busts (high draft picks underperforming)

8. **Player Usage Trends**
   - Minutes trends (increasing/decreasing)
   - Usage rate changes
   - Games played per week

9. **Streaming Analysis**
   - Teams that stream positions effectively
   - Best streaming positions
   - Games added vs games dropped ratio

### Matchup & Schedule Analytics
10. **Schedule Difficulty**
    - Strength of schedule remaining
    - Playoff matchup previews
    - Weeks with most/fewest games

11. **Matchup Predictions**
    - Projected category wins based on season averages
    - Win probability for each category
    - Key categories to focus on for upcoming matchups

12. **Weekly Performance vs Season Average**
    - Did teams over/underperform their season averages?
    - Which teams had outlier weeks (good or bad)

### Transaction Analytics
13. **Transaction Impact**
    - Impact of adds/drops on team performance
    - Best/worst transactions of the season
    - Transaction frequency vs success rate

14. **Trade Analysis**
    - Trade winners/losers
    - Category impact of trades
    - Teams that trade most/least

### Advanced Analytics
15. **Pace & Volume Metrics**
    - Team pace (possessions per game)
    - Volume stats (total shots, total rebounds attempted)
    - Efficiency vs volume trade-offs

16. **Clutch Performance**
    - Performance in close matchups (5-4, 6-3)
    - Comeback wins
    - Blowout wins/losses

17. **Consistency Metrics**
    - Standard deviation of weekly category wins
    - Most consistent teams (low variance)
    - Most volatile teams (high variance)

18. **Playoff Readiness**
    - Teams best positioned for playoffs
    - Playoff schedule analysis
    - Rest days during playoff weeks

### Comparative Analytics
19. **League Rankings by Category**
    - Best team in each category
    - Worst team in each category
    - Category dominance rankings

20. **Team Archetypes**
    - Categorize teams (e.g., "Volume Scorers", "Defensive Specialists", "Balanced")
    - Similar teams analysis
    - Unique team builds

### Predictive Analytics
21. **Rest of Season Projections**
    - Projected final standings
    - Playoff probability
    - Category finish predictions

22. **Player Regression Candidates**
    - Players overperforming (likely to regress)
    - Players underperforming (likely to improve)
    - Breakout candidates

### Visualizations (Future)
23. **Charts & Graphs**
    - Category win trends over time
    - Team performance heatmaps
    - Matchup comparison charts
    - Player performance graphs
